About TorchTracker:

TorchTracker is a straightforward and reliable app designed to help you estimate the calories burned during physical activity. Using the Metabolic Equivalent of Task (MET) formula, it calculates energy expenditure based on your weight (kg), exercise duration, and selected activity. With an easy-to-use interface and a built-in database of MET values for common exercises, TorchTracker provides quick and accurate results to help you stay on top of your fitness goals.

Whether you're going for a walk, cycling, or hitting the gym, TorchTracker makes it easy to track your calorie burn and stay motivated.
